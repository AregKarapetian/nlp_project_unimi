In a small village nestled between two great mountains, there lived a devoted husband and wife who were both skilled artisans. The husband was a master tailor, while his wife was a talented seamstress and an expert housekeeper. One day, their tidy home was disrupted by a series of unfortunate events: pots, glasses, and plates lay shattered on the kitchen floor.

The husband's curiosity got the better of him as he questioned his wife about the destruction. "Who broke these precious items?" he asked, his voice tinged with concern. His wife, her face twisted in anger, replied, "I did it, with my scissors." The husband's eyes narrowed, and he demanded to know if she was telling the truth.

The wife, still seething with emotion, insisted that she had indeed used her scissors to break the household items. Her husband, determined to uncover the truth, decided to test her claim. He tied a sturdy rope around her waist and began to lower her into their well, saying, "Come, let us see if it was truly your scissors that caused this destruction."

As he lowered her deeper into the well, his wife continued to maintain that she had used the scissors. The husband's patience wore thin as he asked her again and again what she had done with the broken items. His wife's obstinacy only grew stronger, until finally, her feet touched the cool water at the bottom of the well.

The husband, enraged by his wife's refusal to confess, let go of the rope, and his wife slipped beneath the surface of the water. But even as she struggled to breathe, she continued to insist that it was indeed the scissors that had broken the household items.

As her husband watched in horror, his wife managed to stick her hand out of the water and began to mimic the motion of cutting with scissors using her fingers. The poor man was torn between his desire to uncover the truth and his love for his wife. He decided to pull her out of the well, hoping that she might finally reveal what had really happened.

As he lifted her out of the water, his wife's eyes met his, but still, she refused to confess. And so, the husband was left with a mystery that would haunt him forever: how had his wife managed to break all those precious items without using the scissors?
